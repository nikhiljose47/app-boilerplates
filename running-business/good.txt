
Start Small, Then Expand

Best strategy:
Dominate a tiny niche → expand outward


Examples:
Facebook → only Harvard first
Amazon → only books first

Lesson:
➡️ Big vision, small starting market.



Founders & Teams

Strong startups need: Small, committed teams --> 5–10 people ideal early

1.Everyone must care deeply
2.Clear ownership
3.Defined roles
4.Strong leadership

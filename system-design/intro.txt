
System design is the process of defining the architecture, components, data flow, and scalability of a software system 
before implementation.


Interview feel - https://www.reddit.com/r/InterviewCoderHQ/comments/1q8aohu/google_em_real_system_design_interview_experience/


Roles of a system designer : Guy who does business req --> materialize report

1.Problem Understanding & Requirement Analysis
2.HLD
3.Data & storage design
4.API integration design
5.Application & Code Architecture
6.Performance & scalability planning
7.Reliability, Fault Tolerance & Resilience
8.Security & Compliance
9.CI/CD & Deployment Strategy
10.Documentation & Communication
11.Collaboration & Technical Leadership - Close with stakeholder, Mentor, Lead tech discussions & design reviews.
12.Continuous Improvement & Evolution - Review, Refactor & Updated on the architecture
